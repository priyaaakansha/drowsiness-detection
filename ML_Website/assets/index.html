Problem Statement

A ML Algorithm to test the sleepiness of the driver and alcohol testing from the sensors deployed on the dashboard/sterring wheel of car.

Read More

Objective

21 percent of all fatal accidents are due to drowsy driving. 60 percent of adult drivers or about 168 million people have driven a vehicle while feeling drowsy in the past year. About 37 percent or 103 million people have fallen asleep at the wheel, according to the National Sleep Foundation's [NSF] 2005 poll.

In order to prevent this, we provide this model & solution.
